Vero Pizza Time

How to play: You start off with 15 cash, you get cash every time you make a delivery to the correct house (The highlighted one). Once you make a delivery you get 5 cash and if you get hit by a car or run out of time you lose 5 cash. If you lose all your money and have 0 cash, you lose (you can still play even if you have 1 cash). Use cash to buy upgrades because the cars get faster as you make more deliveries. Have fun!
